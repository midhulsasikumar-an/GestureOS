1. Issue ID: GI-001 — Palm orientation occasionally misclassified

Observed:

Palm facing camera is sometimes reported as BACK or SIDE.
Misclassification appears to vary with hand angle and lighting.

Current Impact:

Low (Developer Mode only).
Does not currently affect gesture execution because actions are not yet implemented.

Investigate after:

Completion of Checkpoint 4 or before the final gesture/action integration.

Potential Improvements:

Review orientation heuristic.
Tune orientation thresholds.
Add orientation confidence rather than a hard label.
Improve robustness under different lighting and viewing angles.
Validate against front/side/back test cases.
